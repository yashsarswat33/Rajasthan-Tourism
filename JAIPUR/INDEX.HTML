    Explore Jaipur - The Pink City

    Jaipur, the capital city of Rajasthan, is a vibrant blend of old and new. Known as the Pink City due to its distinctly colored buildings, it is home to opulent palaces, ancient forts, and a rich cultural heritage. Experience the grandeur of royal history intertwined with modern charm.

    Quick Information about Jaipur

    Region North India
    Established 1727
    Founder Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh II
    Coordinates 26.9124° N, 75.7873° E
    Major Attractions Amber Fort, Hawa Mahal, City Palace, Jantar Mantar
    Official Language Hindi
    Population 3.1 Million (Approx.)
    Best Time to Visit October to March
    Specialty Traditional Crafts, Handicrafts, Jewelry, Textiles
    Famous Food Dal Baati Churma, Ghewar, Pyaaz Kachori
