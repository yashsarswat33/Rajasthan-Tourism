    Welcome to Rajasthan - Padharo Mhare Desh !

    The land of Maharajas, lakes, grace, emotions, adventure, and vibrancy, Rajasthan with its royal essence welcomes you with all its heart. "Khamma Ghani", it says, and has the beauty that never betrays. Rajasthan is a magical land full of cheerfulness and quintessential charm. In the era where development has taken over, the state remains truthful to its roots and customs. In every street you will find a new ritual, in every face, you'll see an acquaintance. It is a land that showers you with love and a melting pot of multiple religions that is meant to be visited once a lifetime.

    Quick Information about Rajasthan

    Region North India
    Capital Jaipur
    Coordinate 27.0238° N, 74.2179° E
    Total Population 68,548,437 (According to 2011 Census)
    Geographical Area 342,239 Square Kilometres
    Total Districts 33
    Religion Hindu, Muslim, Christian, Sikh, Buddhist, Jain
    Major Tribes Sahariyas, Minas, Bhils, Gadiya Lohars, Garasias..etc
    Languages Spoken Hindi, English, Marwari, Jaipuri/Dhundhari, Malvi/Malwi, Mewati, Urdu..etc
    State Animal Camel & Chinkara
    State Flower Rohida (Tecomella Undulata)
    State Bird Godawan (Great Indian Bustard)
    State Tree Khejri (Prosopis Cineraria)
    State Dance Ghoomar
    State Game Basketball
